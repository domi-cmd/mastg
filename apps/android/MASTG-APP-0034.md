Three separate Android reference apps for OWASP MASTG. Intentionally vulnerable, built as part of a Bachelor thesis for research and teaching. Contain vulnerabilities of the categories MASVS-STORAGE, MASVS-CRYPTO and MASVS-PLATFORM.
